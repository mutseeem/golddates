Nutritional Excellence

The Power of Dates

Dates are a fleshy fruit extremely popular in the Middle East, where they are considered a staple ingredient and part of daily diet. In Algeria, we love consuming dates, fresh or dried, as a snack or integrated into recipes to add even more deliciousness.

Key Characteristics

Dates are an excellent source of dietary fiber, rich in carbohydrates, with antioxidant power. They provide energy quickly and promote digestive well-being.

Health Benefits

Fresh or dried, dates are a good source of dietary fiber. Their high carbohydrate content makes them fruits with high energy content, perfect for intense sports activities. Moreover, they contain a high concentration of antioxidants.

Nutritional Values

Nutrients Dried Dates (25g) Fresh Dates (25g)
Calories 70 kcal 66 kcal
Proteins 0.5g 0.4g
Carbohydrates 16.2g 18.0g
Lipids 0.05g 0.0g
Dietary Fiber 1.8g 1.6g

Cardiovascular Health

Dates help reduce cholesterol levels and contribute to decreasing the risk of cardiovascular diseases.

Perfect for Athletes

Rich in carbohydrates, dates are an ideal food to facilitate muscle work during prolonged efforts.

Antioxidant Power

Fresh dates contain a high concentration of antioxidants that protect body cells.

Digestive Health

Composed of insoluble and soluble fiber, dates play an important role in intestinal regularity.
